ABOUT

Hi! My name is Lorna B.

Hugs & Salsa is a global salsa dance journey inspired from my travels as a community health promoter in Latin America and my dance background and upbringing in Puerto Rico.

The project aims to be a cultural exchange with other dance enthusiasts, regardless of dance discipline.

This is a space to develop a broad network of friends that interconnects energetic and non-conforming individuals, willing to collaborate and share fresh ideas.

Where talents are highlighted through the peered partnership of Salseros en Clave, the #1 social media platform for salsa with over 150,000 followers.

And participant personal growth and inclusivity are valued at the core of the workshop experience.

As a conscientious and dynamic dancer, I care to encourage peer connections, intersectionality, body ownership, and holistic cross-training for my students, to support their unique style and empowered expression.

Lorna Bouret trained in Puerto Rico at Escuela Especializada en Ballet Julián E. Blanco and Academia de Bailes Iveth Mejías. She holds an Anthropology degree from the University of Florida, and has mentored for organizations such as Peace Corps, Iniciativa Comunitaria, Gainesville Girls Rock Camp, and other service-learning institutions. When not traveling, Lorna calls Gainesville, Florida home.
